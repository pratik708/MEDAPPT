FAQ

Common questions

Here are some of the most common questions that we get.
How do I book a medical appointment? To book a medical appointment, simply visit our website and follow the steps outlined on the booking page.
Can I cancel or reschedule my appointment? Yes, you can cancel or reschedule your appointment by logging into your account and accessing the 'My Appointments' section.
What information do I need to provide when booking an appointment? When booking an appointment, you will need to provide your name, contact information, preferred date and time, and any specific medical concerns or requirements.
How far in advance can I book an appointment? You can book an appointment up to 30 days in advance.
Is my personal information secure? Yes, we take the security of your personal information seriously. We use encryption technology to protect your data and adhere to strict privacy policies.
